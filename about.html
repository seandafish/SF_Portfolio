About Me

Hi, I'm Sean Fisher-- a data enthusiast with a passion in sports.

I'm a UC San Diego Transfer Graduate with B.S in Mathematics and B.A in Economics. I transfered from Moorpark College where I earned an A.A in Economics and A.S in Business Administration.

My passion for data began at a young age when I became captivated by analyzing every statistic from my favorite baseball team, the Angels. As I matured, I developed a deeper appreciation for the power of data, from its ability to present valuable insights to its role in predicting future outcomes.

My Technical Skills include: Python (data analysis packages), SQL, Machine Learning, Statistical Analysis, Data Analysis, Data Visulization, Feature Engineering, Data Cleaning, Data Pre-Processing, Data Modeling, Computational Skills, and LaTeX.
